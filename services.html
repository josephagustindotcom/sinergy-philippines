Our Process

Services only work when the process behind them is clear.

Sinergy does not approach outsourcing as simple task handoff. We help define the role, train around the workflow, launch with clarity, and improve based on performance.

1

3

4

Launch

5

Improve

We review performance and refine the process as your needs grow or change.
