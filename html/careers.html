Hiring Process

How the application process works.

The hiring process may vary depending on the role, but Sinergy generally looks for communication ability, reliability, role fit, and willingness to learn.

1

Submit Application

Send your resume and role interest so the team can review your background.

2

Initial Screening

We review communication skills, experience, availability, and role fit.

3

Skills Review

Depending on the role, you may complete a short task, interview, or practical assessment.

4

Training & Onboarding

Selected candidates receive role-specific guidance before working with client workflows.

How to Apply

Send a focused application, not a lazy one.

Weak applications look generic. Strong applications clearly show what role you want, what experience you have, and why you are a good fit for client support work.

1. Include your target role Example: Customer Support, Technical Support, Data Entry Specialist, Sales Support, or Data Annotation.
2. Attach your updated resume Highlight relevant tools, work experience, communication skills, and achievements.
3. Add a short introduction Explain why you are reliable, trainable, and a strong fit for the role.
