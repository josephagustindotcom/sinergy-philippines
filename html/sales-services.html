Why It Works

Sales support is not a replacement for your closers. It protects their time.

The best use of outsourced sales support is not pretending offshore agents will magically close everything. The stronger play is using them to keep prospecting, follow-up, and admin work consistent.

Keep lead generation, outbound calling, and follow-up moving even when your internal team is busy.
