Process Overview

Outsourcing works best when the process is built before the team scales.

The weak version of outsourcing is hiring quickly without documenting the work. Sinergy uses a more structured approach: define the work, match the right team, train carefully, then improve through review.

🔍

Discovery & Requirements

  • Service needs
  • Work volume
  • Tools and systems
  • Role expectations
🧭

Workflow Mapping

  • Task ownership
  • Escalation paths
  • Response standards
  • Quality criteria

Detailed Setup Flow

What happens at each stage.

This is the operating structure. Do not skip the workflow work. That is where most outsourcing setups fail.

01

Discovery Call

We clarify what you need and whether outsourcing is the right fit.

We identify:

Business goals
Current team gaps
Service type needed
Expected workload
Preferred communication setup
Urgency and launch timeline
Output:

A clearer understanding of the role, workload, and outsourcing opportunity.

02

Workflow Planning

We convert your needs into tasks, roles, responsibilities, and operating rules.

We map:

Daily responsibilities
Standard procedures
Escalation rules
Tools and access needs
Quality control points
Reporting rhythm
Output:

A practical role structure and workflow plan before hiring or launching the team.

03

Team Matching

We match people to the role based on the type of work and required skills.

We consider:

Communication ability
Customer-facing experience
Technical familiarity
Attention to detail
Sales support capability
Trainability and reliability
Output:

A team profile designed around the actual work, not generic hiring assumptions.

04

Training & Launch

The team learns your business, tools, tone, process, and quality expectations.

Training may include:

Brand voice
Product or service knowledge
CRM or helpdesk tools
Support scripts and FAQs
Sample tickets or tasks
Escalation scenarios
Output:

A prepared team ready to start with supervision, review, and feedback.

05

Management Rhythm

Once live, the team needs communication, reporting, and quality review.

We support:

Daily or weekly check-ins
Performance tracking
Task completion review
Issue escalation
Output quality checks
Workflow improvements
Output:

An operating rhythm that keeps the team accountable and easier to manage.

Engagement Models

Start with the setup that matches your current workload.

The right model depends on your workload, budget, urgency, and how much internal management capacity you have.

Starter

Small Support Team

Best for businesses starting to outsource one clear function.

  • One service area
  • Defined daily tasks
  • Simple reporting
  • Good for first-time outsourcing
Most Flexible

Dedicated Team

Best for businesses with steady workload and ongoing support needs.

  • Dedicated offshore team members
  • Client-specific training
  • Ongoing management rhythm
  • Scalable as workload grows
Scale

Multi-Function Team

Best for companies that need support across multiple departments.

  • Customer support plus data work
  • Sales support plus CRM admin
  • Technical support plus escalation
  • More structured reporting
Client Preparation

Outsourcing becomes stronger when the client provides clear inputs.

A team cannot perform well if the business gives vague expectations, no documentation, no access, and no feedback. The setup needs collaboration from both sides.

Prepare Your Team Setup
1

Clear Role Expectations

Define what the team should handle, what they should not handle, and when they should escalate.

2

Tools and Access

Prepare the systems, accounts, permissions, templates, inboxes, CRMs, or helpdesk platforms needed.

3

Training Materials

Provide FAQs, scripts, product information, brand rules, sample responses, and workflow examples.

4

Feedback Rhythm

Review early work, correct issues fast, and help the team improve during the launch period.

Quality & Accountability

How we keep the team aligned after launch.

Launch is not the finish line. The team needs monitoring, feedback, reporting, and ongoing workflow improvement.

📊

Reporting

Track activity, output, completed tasks, pending items, issues, and improvement areas.

✅

Quality Review

Review responses, data output, tickets, calls, labels, or completed tasks against standards.

🔁

Feedback Loops

Use client feedback and internal review to improve scripts, workflows, and team performance.

📈

Scaling Plan

Expand team size, coverage, or service scope once the process is working reliably.
